About Le Nid à Paris

Le Nid à Paris was created for owners who love their Paris apartment but cannot always be here. It is a boutique, human-scale concierge service rooted in trust, clarity, and a calm way of working.

I’m Amanda, the founder of Le Nid à Paris. Originally from Pennsylvania, I moved to Paris over twelve years ago and built a life here with my family. I hold a master’s degree from Columbia University and spent years working in bilingual client relations and project coordination.

Over time, I met many pied-à-terre owners who loved their Paris home but felt anxious about managing it from afar. I created Le Nid à Paris to offer a calm, trustworthy presence — someone who knows your apartment, your habits, and your expectations.

A boutique, human-scale service.

I work with a limited number of apartments at a time. This is a deliberate choice, so that every visit, every report, and every coordination is done personally, without rushing.

Communication is in English or French, depending on your preference. I adapt to the way you like to be updated: detailed reports or short notes, regular check-ins or only when something needs a decision.

The name “Le Nid à Paris” reflects what I hope your apartment feels like to you: a quiet nest in the city, well cared for, waiting for you.
