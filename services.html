Services

Le Nid à Paris offers a focused set of services designed specifically for pied-à-terre owners. The aim is simple: your apartment remains cared for, ready, and calm — without you needing to manage every detail from afar.

Monthly oversight

Regular visits & detailed eyes.

  • Visual inspection of all rooms, windows, and doors
  • Airing the apartment and checking basic utilities
  • Mail collection and scanning of important letters
  • Short written report with photos after each visit
  • Alert in case of any anomaly or concern
Cleaning coordination

A ready-to-live-in apartment.

  • Scheduling trusted cleaning professionals
  • Opening and closing the apartment if needed
  • Quality check after cleaning
  • Coordination of laundry (bed linens, towels)
  • Restocking basic essentials upon request
Repair & maintenance coordination

Support when something needs attention.

  • Organizing artisans (plumber, locksmith, electrician, etc.)
  • Being present during interventions when required
  • Obtaining and sharing quotes for your approval
  • Follow-up until the work is completed
  • Emergency visits in case of urgent issues
Check-in / Check-out

Welcoming guests or tenants in person.

  • Greeting guests or tenants on arrival
  • Key handover and apartment walkthrough
  • Explanation of building rules and key equipment
  • Check-out inspection at the end of the stay
  • Reporting back to you if anything needs attention
Optional add-ons

Small gestures, big comfort.

  • Pre-arrival grocery stocking
  • Plant care during longer absences
  • Seasonal deep cleaning coordination
  • Administrative assistance with syndic or insurance
A boutique approach

Limited number of apartments.

To maintain a calm, attentive service, I work with a limited number of homes at the same time. This allows me to follow each apartment personally and respond quickly when you or your guests need support.

Services can be combined and adapted to your needs. We will define together the right level of involvement for your pied-à-terre.
